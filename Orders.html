Ordering Information

How to Place an Order

To place a catering order, please follow these steps:

  1. Browse our menus and choose your desired items.
  2. Send us an email at orders@dignanscatering.com with the following information:
    • Your name and contact details
    • Selected menu items and quantities
    • Event date and time
    • Special instructions or dietary requirements
  3. Wait for a confirmation email from our team with the order details and total cost.

User Registration

Order Processing Time

Please place your orders at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability and timely delivery. For larger events, we recommend contacting us at least one week prior.
