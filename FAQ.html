Frequently Asked Questions

What catering services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of catering services including office lunches, corporate events, celebration buffets, and more. You can find detailed information on our Menus page.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by sending us an email at orders@dignanscatering.com with your requirements. For more details, visit our Ordering Information page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments. Payment details will be provided upon order confirmation.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the event are fully refundable. For more details, please contact us directly.

Do you offer vegetarian or special dietary options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please let us know your dietary requirements when placing your order.

Do you provide cutlery and disposables?

Yes, we provide all necessary disposables including plates, cutlery, and napkins at no extra charge.
